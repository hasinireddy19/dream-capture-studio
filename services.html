Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your session, timelines, and deliverables.

What is your standard gallery delivery timeline?

+

For portrait and fashion sessions, you will receive your final edited gallery within 3-4 weeks. For weddings, delivery is typically 6-8 weeks due to the extensive curation and high-volume retouching involved. We also offer an Express Delivery add-on if you need them sooner!

Do you travel for out-of-town weddings or shoots?

+

Yes, we love traveling to capture beautiful stories! Travel expenses and accommodations are calculated based on the location and will be added transparently to the custom quote. Reach out via the contact form to discuss travel details.

Can we request raw/unedited image files?

+

We do not deliver raw or unedited files. Curation, color grading, and custom professional editing are a vital part of our artistic signature and look. The high-resolution edited files in your gallery are complete and ready for printing.

What happens if it rains on our scheduled shoot day?

+

For outdoor sessions, we monitor weather forecasts closely. If heavy rain or extreme conditions are expected, we will coordinate with you to reschedule to the next available backup date at no extra charge. We want your photos to look bright, warm, and beautiful!
